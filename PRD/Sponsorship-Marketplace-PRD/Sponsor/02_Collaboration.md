# 10. Tasks

## Purpose

The Tasks module enables teams to assign, track, prioritize, and complete work items related to sponsorship opportunities, negotiations, campaigns, financial activities, and reporting.

Tasks ensure accountability throughout the sponsorship lifecycle by assigning clear ownership, deadlines, dependencies, and completion status.

---

## Task Sources

Tasks may be created from:

- Opportunity Review
- Internal Evaluation
- Sponsorship Application
- Negotiation
- Contract Review
- Budget Approval
- Campaign Milestone
- Deliverable Review
- Brand Asset Approval
- Executive Decision
- Analytics Review
- Manual Task Creation

---

## Task Types

### Review Task

Review sponsorship opportunities, contracts, budgets, or reports.

---

### Approval Task

Approve documents, budgets, campaigns, or contracts.

---

### Follow-up Task

Follow up with organizers, partners, or internal stakeholders.

---

### Document Task

Upload, verify, or review required documentation.

---

### Campaign Task

Execute campaign activities and sponsorship deliverables.

---

### Financial Task

Manage invoices, payments, and budget activities.

---

### Compliance Task

Complete legal, tax, or governance requirements.

---

## Task Information

Each task includes:

- Task ID
- Title
- Description
- Related Module
- Related Record
- Priority
- Assigned To
- Assigned By
- Due Date
- Status
- Progress
- Estimated Effort
- Attachments
- Comments

---

## Task Priority

- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

---

## Task Status

- Draft
- Open
- Assigned
- In Progress
- Waiting
- Blocked
- Completed
- Cancelled
- Archived

## Task Dashboard

## Task Features

# 11. Internal Approvals

## Purpose

The Internal Approvals module manages structured approval workflows for sponsorship decisions across departments, ensuring governance, compliance, and accountability.

Approval workflows are configurable based on business rules, investment thresholds, organizational hierarchy, and document types.

---

## Approval Scope

Internal approvals support:

- Opportunity Selection
- Sponsorship Applications
- Budget Approvals
- Commercial Terms
- Contracts
- Brand Assets
- Campaign Launch
- Deliverables
- Financial Payments
- Executive Reports

---

## Approval Workflow

```text
Request Submitted
        │
        ▼
Department Review
        │
        ▼
Functional Approval
        │
        ▼
Finance Review
        │
        ▼
Legal Review
        │
        ▼
Executive Approval
        │
        ▼
Approved / Rejected
```

# 12. Activity Timeline

## Purpose

The Activity Timeline provides a chronological record of every significant action performed within the Sponsor Workspace.

It serves as both an operational history and an audit-friendly activity feed, enabling users to quickly understand what happened, who performed the action, and when it occurred.

---

## Timeline Sources

Activities originate from:

- Opportunity Management
- Applications
- Negotiations
- Deal Management
- Campaign Management
- Financial Management
- Analytics
- Collaboration
- Administration

---

## Timeline Events

Examples include:

- Opportunity Saved
- Opportunity Compared
- Application Submitted
- Offer Sent
- Contract Signed
- Budget Approved
- Invoice Generated
- Payment Processed
- Deliverable Approved
- Report Published
- User Mentioned
- Task Completed

---

## Activity Record

Each activity contains:

- Activity ID
- Event Type
- Module
- Related Record
- User
- Timestamp

- Previous State
- Current State
