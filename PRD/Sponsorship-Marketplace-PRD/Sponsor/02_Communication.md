# 8. Message Center

## Purpose

The Message Center provides a centralized, contextual messaging system for sponsor organizations to communicate internally and externally throughout the sponsorship lifecycle.

Unlike comments (which are attached to records), the Message Center supports persistent conversations, direct messaging, group discussions, project channels, and communication with organizers, partners, and authorized external stakeholders.

All conversations are permission-aware, searchable, auditable, and linked to relevant business entities.

---

## Messaging Scope

The Message Center supports communication between:

- Internal Team Members
- Department Teams
- Sponsorship Managers
- Marketing Teams
- Finance Teams
- Procurement Teams
- Legal Teams
- Executives
- Organizer Representatives
- Partner Representatives
- External Collaborators

---

## Conversation Types

### Direct Messages

One-to-one conversations between users.

---

### Group Conversations

Multiple participants collaborating on a topic.

---

### Campaign Channels

Dedicated communication channels for campaigns.

Examples:

- Tech Summit 2027
- Sports Sponsorship FY27
- CSR Initiatives

---

### Opportunity Discussions

Conversations linked to sponsorship opportunities.

---

### Negotiation Conversations

Private communication during commercial negotiations.

---

### Financial Conversations

Budget, invoice, payment, and contract discussions.

---

### Executive Discussions

Restricted conversations for executive decision-making.

# 9. Announcement Center

## Purpose

The Announcement Center enables organizations to publish important organizational communications, executive updates, operational notices, campaign announcements, maintenance notifications, and policy changes.

Announcements provide one-to-many communication with controlled visibility, scheduling, acknowledgement tracking, and expiration.

## Acknowledgement Workflow

Some announcements require acknowledgement.

Workflow:

```text
Announcement Published
        │
        ▼
User Receives Notification
        │
        ▼
User Opens Announcement
        │
        ▼
Acknowledgement Required?
        │
      Yes
        │

User Confirms
        │
        ▼
Acknowledgement Recorded
```
