# 10. Deliverables

## Purpose

Deliverables represent every contractual obligation that the organizer must provide to the sponsor throughout the campaign lifecycle.

The Deliverables module acts as the operational execution tracker, ensuring every sponsorship benefit defined in the contract is planned, monitored, approved, and completed.

Rather than manually tracking deliverables in spreadsheets, every deliverable is managed with ownership, due dates, approvals, supporting evidence, and audit history.

# 11. Deliverable Approval

## Purpose

Ensures that every contractual deliverable is verified by the sponsor before being marked complete.

---

## Approval Workflow

```text
Deliverable Assigned
        │
        ▼
Organizer Submission
        │
        ▼
Evidence Uploaded
        │
        ▼
Sponsor Review
        │
        ▼
Approve
   │          │
   ▼          ▼
Completed   Revision Requested
```
