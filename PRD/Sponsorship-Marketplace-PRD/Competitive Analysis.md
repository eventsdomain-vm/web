# 2. Market Overview

The sponsorship industry lacks a dominant end-to-end platform.

Today organizations typically combine multiple tools to manage sponsorships:

• Marketplace websites

↓

• CRM

↓

• Email

↓

• Spreadsheets

↓

• Contracts

↓

• Project Management

↓

• Analytics

↓

• Finance

This fragmented workflow creates:

- Duplicate data
- Lost opportunities
- Poor visibility
- Manual work
- Low collaboration

SMP aims to unify these workflows into a single platform.

# 12. Strategic Conclusion

Most competitors solve only one part of the sponsorship lifecycle.

SMP's strategy is to become the single platform where organizers, sponsors, agencies, and administrators manage every stage of sponsorship—from opportunity discovery to post-event ROI reporting.

This unified approach reduces operational complexity, improves transparency, and creates a stronger long-term competitive advantage than standalone marketplace or CRM solutions.
