# 1. Purpose

This document evaluates the sponsorship technology market, identifies competitors, analyzes their strengths and weaknesses, and defines the strategic positioning of the Sponsorship Marketplace Platform (SMP).

The objective is not to replicate existing products but to identify opportunities for innovation and differentiation.

---

# 2. Industry Overview

The sponsorship industry is undergoing digital transformation.

Traditionally, sponsorship acquisition and management rely on:

- Email outreach
- Phone calls
- Excel spreadsheets
- PDF sponsorship decks
- Manual negotiations
- Offline relationship management

Modern organizations increasingly expect centralized platforms that support:

- Sponsor discovery
- Marketplace search
- CRM
- Workflow automation
- Analytics
- Contracts
- Payments
- Team collaboration

# 7. SWOT Analysis

## Strengths

- Unified sponsorship lifecycle
- Marketplace + CRM
- Enterprise architecture
- AI recommendations
- Modular platform

---

## Weaknesses

- Requires marketplace liquidity
- Higher implementation complexity
- Longer onboarding for enterprise customers

---

## Opportunities

- Untapped SMB organizer market
- Digital transformation of sponsorship sales
- AI-assisted sponsorship matching
- White-label enterprise solutions
- API ecosystem

---

## Threats

- Established CRM vendors
- New marketplace entrants
- Economic downturn affecting sponsorship budgets
- Platform disintermediation (direct organizer-sponsor relationships)

---

# 8. Market Gaps

Current platforms generally lack:

- End-to-end sponsorship lifecycle management
- Integrated CRM
- Deliverable tracking
- AI-powered recommendations
- Enterprise RBAC
- Workflow automation
- Unified analytics
- Contract lifecycle management

These gaps represent SMP's primary opportunities.

# 13. Success Criteria

The platform will be considered competitively successful when it:

- Attracts verified organizers and sponsors.
- Increases sponsorship conversion rates.
- Reduces manual administrative work.
- Improves visibility into sponsorship performance.
- Supports organizations ranging from startups to enterprises.
- Establishes a sustainable recurring revenue model.
